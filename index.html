Flagship build · Pocket Defender

An AI platform that runs a law practice

A comprehensive, AI-powered case & client management platform built specifically for criminal defense attorneys. I designed it, built it, and run it as founder — and use it daily in my own practice, so every feature earns its place against a real caseload. This is what "custom AI software" looks like when it ships.

  • Caseload at a glance — a live dashboard of every client, case number, and hearing, color-coded so nothing slips before a court date.
  • Daily Docket — work each hearing from one screen: charges, defendant details, plea tracking, AI discovery summaries, and notes side by side.
  • AI document generation — draft court-ready filings auto-filled from the case file, then review in a clean preview before it's final.
  • AI extraction & agent access — pull client info and summarize discovery from documents and screenshots, and connect Claude to act on cases.
  • Built to run a firm — secure client portal, two-way messaging, automated tasks, staff accounts, calendar & drive sync, payments, and reporting.
